What is the difference between ChatGPT and Claude?

ChatGPT is made by OpenAI and Claude is made by Anthropic. Both are AI assistants you can chat with. ChatGPT is available as a $20/month subscription (ChatGPT Plus) or via API (pay per use). Claude is also available as a subscription or API. The best choice depends on your specific task — describe your idea above and we'll tell you which fits better.

What does "API pricing" mean for AI tools?

API pricing means you pay based on how much you use the tool — like a phone bill. Subscription pricing means you pay a flat monthly fee — like Netflix. For most small business owners starting out, a subscription is more predictable. API pricing makes sense when you need to build something custom or use AI inside your own product.
